Alex vs. the Internet

William Alexander Coble

They said I hated the Internet. They said I'd never have a website. Look at me now.

Where should I start? I was born on February 27th, 1992 in Atlanta. I’ve floated around a bit between Stone Mountain, Chamblee, the West End and Grant Park but have never managed to escape the city for more than a couple weeks. I enrolled at Morehouse College in 2010 as an English major, hoping I could leverage the degree to become a screenwriter. After some years off following 2012 and a few key revelations, I returned to Morehouse in the Spring of 2014 to take my first course in Computer Science. Since then I’ve been hooked on the degree’s problem solving imperatives and employability.
